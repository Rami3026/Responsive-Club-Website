Welcome to Friends desustain International

Friends desustain International focuses on education to the less privileged, economic empowerment and environment or health and social amenities.
Friends desustain International supports its beneficiaries within Western Region of Ghana and beyond. Our focus is to support deprived basic schools and rural communities .
